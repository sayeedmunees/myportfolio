About me

A passionate Front-end developer with a degree in B.Tech in Electronics and Communication, with a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and knowledge of Python, Dart, C, and MySQL.

Skilled in design tools like Photoshop, Adobe XD, and Figma, with a keen eye for blending aesthetics and functionality.

Committed to crafting immersive user experiences and dynamic web applications, eager to contribute creativity and technical expertise to a forward-thinking team that values innovation in front-end development

Projects

Bunkscape - Attendance Calculator

Designed and developed attendance calculator that helps students find out how many days they can bunk or the minimum number of days they need to attend to maintain their attendance percentage.

Mercedes-AMG G 63 Landing Page

Designed and developed a sleek landing page prototype for the Mercedes-AMG G 63, built using vanilla CSS with Flexbox. The design highlights the power and elegance of this iconic vehicle.

Concept SG90 Armbot

Designed and developed a website using HTML, CSS, JavaScript as part of our academic project named Armbot. The Armbot is designed for warehouse automation.
